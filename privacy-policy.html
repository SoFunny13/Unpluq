Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Unpluq ("we", "our", or "the app") is a digital wellness application designed to help you reduce screen time and build healthier phone habits. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

Usage Analytics

  • App interaction events (screens viewed, features used, session duration)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Anonymous user identifier (generated locally on your device)

Attribution Data

  • Install source and advertising campaign information
  • This data helps us understand how users discover our app

Purchase Information

  • Subscription status and purchase history (processed through Apple's App Store)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To measure the effectiveness of our marketing efforts
  • To manage subscriptions and in-app purchases

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Mixpanel — analytics platform for understanding app usage patterns
  • AppsFlyer — mobile attribution platform for measuring install sources
  • Adapty — subscription management platform for processing in-app purchases

These services may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or messages
  • We do not track which specific apps you use (Screen Time data stays on your device and is managed by Apple's frameworks)
  • We do not sell your data to third parties

5. Data Storage and Security

Your personal settings and focus session data are stored locally on your device. Analytics data is transmitted securely via HTTPS to our third-party service providers.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete the app at any time, which removes all locally stored data
  • Request deletion of your analytics data by contacting us
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by disabling App Tracking in iOS Settings

7. Children's Privacy

Unpluq is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
