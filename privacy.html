Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 27, 2026

Unpluq ("we", "our", "the app") is a digital wellbeing application that helps you manage screen time and build healthier phone habits. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Data We Collect

Unpluq processes the following data entirely on your device:

  • App usage categories and screen time statistics (via Apple Screen Time API)
  • Focus session history (duration, completion status)
  • Your chosen app blocking preferences
  • Schedule and goal settings

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of this data on external servers. All data remains on your device and in your personal iCloud account (if iCloud sync is enabled by your device settings).

2. App Names and Identifiers

Unpluq uses Apple's FamilyControls and DeviceActivity frameworks. These frameworks provide opaque tokens that represent apps — we never see or store actual app names, bundle identifiers, or usage details.

3. Subscription and Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details.

4. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, Unpluq sends local notifications (daily digest, goal alerts). These are generated on-device and are never routed through external servers.

5. Analytics and Tracking

Unpluq does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not track you across apps or websites.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties. Period.

7. Data Storage and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms. Deleting the app removes all associated data. No data remains on any server because no data was ever sent to one.

8. Children's Privacy

Unpluq is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@unpluq.app.
