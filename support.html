Frequently Asked Questions

How does Unpluq block apps?

Unpluq uses Apple's Screen Time API (FamilyControls framework) to manage app access. Your app usage data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings tab in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Your subscription is linked to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Unpluq → Cancel Subscription.

The app isn't blocking apps correctly. What should I do?

Make sure you've granted Screen Time permissions to Unpluq. Go to iPhone Settings → Screen Time → and ensure Unpluq has the necessary access.

How do I change which apps are blocked?

Open the Settings tab in Unpluq and tap on "Blocked Apps" to modify your selection.
